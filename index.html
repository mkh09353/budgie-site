Why a budgie?

Small bird. Strong opinions.

🏠

Nothing you say ever leaves your Mac.

Budgie runs open speech models (NVIDIA Parakeet, via parakeet.cpp) entirely on your machine. No cloud, no account, no audio uploads, no telemetry. Pull the Wi-Fi cable out — it keeps working.

⚡

Fast as a flap

The engine stays warm in memory, so dictation starts the instant you press the key — and words stream out as you speak.

⌨️

Push-to-talk, not push-your-luck

Hold right ⌘ to record, release to finish. A quick tap keeps the key's normal behaviour — Budgie only listens while you hold.

🪶

A bird, not a behemoth

A native Swift menu-bar app. No Electron, no browser in a trench coat. It perches quietly until you need it.

🛠️

Open source, naturally

Every line is on GitHub. Build it yourself, fork it, teach it new tricks.

git clone github.com/mkh09353/budgie
✨

Two dictation styles

Live types words as you say them. Punctuated waits a beat, then delivers polished text with punctuation.

📋

Your words, your way

Type straight at the cursor in any app, or send the transcript to your clipboard instead. Your choice.

How it works

Three seconds to learn. Honestly.

⌘1

Hold

Press and hold right ⌘ (or pick another key). The bird turns into little red level bars.

🗣️2

Speak

Say the email, the commit message, the grocery list. Words appear at your cursor as you talk.

🎉3

Release

Let go. Done. Budgie even tracks how much typing time you've saved (it adds up fast).

Free as in bird.

No subscription. No "pro tier". No analytics SDK quietly phoning home. Budgie is a weekend-sized open-source app that does one thing beautifully — and you can read every line of how it does it.
